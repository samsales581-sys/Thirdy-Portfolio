About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I am Samuel C. Sales Jr., but you can call me Thirdy. I may seem quiet at first because I am naturally shy and more of an introvert, especially in public. I'm not always the loudest person in the room, but I'm someone who observes deeply, listens carefully, and values genuine connections over small talk.

In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym and playing online games. The gym is more than just a hobby for me—it's a place where I build not only physical strength but also discipline, and focus. Playing online games, on the other hand, helps me relax, think strategically, and connect with friends in a different way.

Above all, my faith in God is the foundation of who I am. I strongly believe that everything happens for a reason and that God has a purpose behind every situation—whether it's a good day or a challenging one. During difficult times, I remind myself that there is always a greater plan at work. This belief gives me peace, strength, and hope to keep moving forward no matter what life brings.

I may be quiet, but I carry strong faith, deep thoughts, and big dreams within me.

My Motivation

I am deeply motivated by the presence of this amazing person in my life who has always been there for me at my lowest moments. She has this rare ability to lift my spirit whenever I feel down, reminding me that I am capable of becoming better than I was yesterday.

Her patience, kindness, and understanding inspire me to keep improving myself, not only for her but also for the person I want to become. No matter what mistakes I have made or how I may have hurt her in the past, she remains the reason why I keep moving forward in life with hope.

And I hold on to this promise in my heart:

"With God as our guide and faith as our foundation, someday we will fulfill our dreams together, walking hand in hand toward the future He has prepared for us."
